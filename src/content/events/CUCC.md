Time Control: Game in 90 minutes with a 30-seconds increment from move 1.

Format: 5 round Swiss (If there are 6 participants or less: round-robin)

Dalhousie is putting a team together that can represent our university at the CUCC (Canadian University Chess Championship). The top 4 players will be qualified to compete at the CUCC. For this reason, this tournament will be open to Dalhousie students only.
