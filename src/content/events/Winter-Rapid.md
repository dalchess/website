Time Control: Game in 25 minutes with a 5-seconds delay from move 1.

Format: Swiss

This will be an over-the-board tournament open to anyone who wants to play other local players. The winner of this tournament will be qualified to play in the Dalhousie Chess Championship, which will be hosted in March.
