Time Control: Game in 90 minutes with a 30-seconds increment from move 1.

Format: 5 round Swiss

This will be an over-the-board CFC-rated classical tournament. The top 2 players from this tournament will be qualified to play in the Dalhousie Chess Championship, which will be hosted in March.
